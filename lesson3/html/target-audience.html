Target Audience

The target audience of the Cast-City Chamber of Commerce and Business web site is described below:

  • Who: Business operators, Firms, Stakeholders and others interested in understanding the Service we offer
  • Age: 18 to 100 (any business owner or stakeholder between the age range)
  • Technology: Modern and legacy solutions, Business organizations, Stake holders, IT professionals involved in the design and implementation of Enterprise Solutions, large integrations, large enterprise infrastructures
  • Motivation: Improve knowledge of Business, technologies and practical application of creating job opportunities in the city. Improve resume and employability.

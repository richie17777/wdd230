Site Plan

Site Description

The purpose of this document is to describe the Kasoa Chamber of Commerce and Business website, its purpose and intended audience, utilization scenarios, and other aspects such as its organization and structure, elements of style, etc. It is to be used as a planning and elaboration tool for the personal site to be developed this semester as part of the WDD 230 course.

Site Name

This site is going to be called Kasoa Chamber of Commerce and Business. As the name indicates, it will be a repository of knowledge regarding business and commerce that will enhance development in the Kasoa area.

Site purpose

The Cast-City Chamber of Commerce and Business is an association of business operators, firms, and industries with interests spanning every sector of private enterprise in Kasoa. As an advocacy organisation, the Cast-City Chamber of Commerce and Business will be established with the prime objective of promoting and protecting commercial and industrial interests in the city.

The Chamber represents the voice of the business community with total commitment to playing a leadership role towards propelling the city's economy to higher heights. As an autonomous business support organisation, we serve as a link between established businesses, the emerging sector, and government at the various levels while offering our members a myriad of business support services.

Target Audience

The target audience of the Kasoa Chamber of Commerce and Business web site is described below:

  • Who: Business operators, Firms, Stakeholders and others interested in understanding the Service we offer
  • Age: 18 to 100 (any business owner or stakeholder between the age range)
  • Technology: Modern and legacy solutions, Business organizations, Stake holders, IT professionals involved in the design and implementation of Enterprise Solutions, large integrations, large enterprise infrastructures
  • Motivation: Improve knowledge of Business, technologies and practical application of creating job opportunities in the city. Improve resume and employability.

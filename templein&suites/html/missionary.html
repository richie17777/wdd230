An inside view of a single room

The TempleInn Luxury Suites is the best Cache Valley lodging, thanks to a vibe that's chic and sophisticated. Comfortable yet stylish, granting easy access to the best Logan has to offer. Architectural & Interior design by Andrea McCulloch of Milieu Design.

The Confort of our double bed rooms

In this hotel a single room has one or two beds with a vanity area and separate bathroom with toilet and tub/shower. I haven't stayed in a suite here but at other places it has a room like a living room with a small kitchenette and dinette. Bedrooms are separate with doors for privacy.

An outsite view of TempleInn

In this area ceremonies are often on the covered verandah and the reception are often in the stable. The verandah had a beautiful view of the surrounding fields, trees, and young vines. It is always opened on the sides, so the cross breeze makes early evening summer temps very comfortable. The stable area is nicely adapted as an event space, almost too-much so, as it doesn't have a very rustic atmosphere. Aside from that and some other minor event management glitches, I would highly recommend this as a location for a special event.
